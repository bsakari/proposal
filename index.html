Android Mobile Bootcamp

About the Bootcamp

The 2-day Android bootcamp provides accelerated training in app development for Entry Level android developers. I have designed the course to cover the core topics required to build Android apps according to the current industry best practices through the following components:

  • Day One Morning session to review key Android concepts, topics and User Interface Design. Running the First Apps.
  • Afternoon session of Material Design and Introduction to Intents.
  • Day Two Sessions of Hands On creation of a complete Android App and Backing it Up to GitHub.
  • Follow Up.

At the end of the program, each student will have created a standard working android app that will have given Him/Her a through thought of how to create fascinating Android Apps.
